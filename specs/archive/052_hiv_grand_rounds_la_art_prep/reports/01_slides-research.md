## Executive Summary

This presentation covers the MXM clinic's long-acting ART (n=34) and PrEP (n=68) programs, showcasing 100% viral suppression outcomes among people experiencing homelessness. The talk uses four patient cases with Poll Everywhere audience interaction to teach clinical decision-making around resistance, LTFU risk, lenacapavir pharmacology, and IRIS considerations. **Content**:

What we did: **D -- Administered LEN + CAB that day**

- In low-barrier settings, act with the opportunity in front of you

Waiting for perfect data means waiting forever. Second: lenacapavir likely protected him. LEN has a longer PK tail than CAB.
